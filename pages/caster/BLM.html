Important

Umbral Ice: Umbral Ice is a buff that significantly increases MP regeneration while lowering the damage and MP cost of Fire spells. At 3 stacks, the cast time of Fire spells is also halved.

Astral Fire: Astral Fire is a buff that increases the damage and MP cost of Fire spells, while lowering the damage and MP cost of Ice spells. At 3 stacks, the cast time of Ice spells is also halved. Natural MP regeneration is halted during Astral Fire. Lucid Dreaming does not work. Manafont and ethers work
